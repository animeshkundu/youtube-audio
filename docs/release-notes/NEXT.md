# YouTube Audio, just the sound

Version: (set on release)

YouTube Audio plays only the audio of YouTube and YouTube Music, so your battery and your mobile data last a lot longer. Install it, and the tab you never look at stops shovelling video down your connection. The music keeps going.

Works on Firefox, desktop and Android. No account, no sign-in, nothing to configure to get started.

## What you get

- **Audio only, video bytes gone.** We point the player at a direct audio stream and stop the video download at the source. Same native controls, far less battery and data.
- **Keeps playing in the background.** Lock the phone, switch tabs, the audio carries on, with your usual lock-screen and OS media controls.
- **Ghost mode.** Ads and tracking pings get blocked before they leave your browser, quietly, by default.
- **Skip the boring bits.** SponsorBlock-style auto-skip past sponsor reads and off-topic music intros. Only a tiny 4-character hash ever leaves your machine to look them up.
- **YouTube Music, leveled up.** Even out loud-and-quiet tracks with loudness normalization, shape the sound with a 5-band equalizer, and follow along with time-synced lyrics.
- **Save a track.** Download the current audio as a single, tidy `.m4a` file that plays just about anywhere.
- **Tidy up the page.** Optionally hide Shorts, recommendations, or comments, cap the quality to save more data, or switch off autoplay-next.

Everything above is one click away in the popup or options. The essentials are on out of the box, the rest waits until you ask.

## What's better in this release

- **Toggling audio-only is rock solid now.** Turn it off and you land right back on the video at the exact spot you were listening to, playing or paused. No frozen player, no jump to the start, even if you flip it on and off in a hurry.
- **Smoother, lighter on your machine.** We tracked down a rare case where the extension could make YouTube feel sluggish and put a stop to it. It stays out of the way now.
- **The popup tells you the truth.** The status you see is the real state of the current video, so when something falls back to normal YouTube, you know, instead of being told all is well.
- **Nicer in the player.** The audio-only button now matches YouTube's own controls in size and style, and audio mode shows the track artwork instead of a black rectangle.
- **Hiding recommendations leaves your comments alone.** Ask to hide recommendations and that is all that goes. Comments stay put.
- **Cleaner downloads.** One track, one well-behaved `.m4a`, no pile of stray files.
- **Lyrics that behave.** On YouTube Music you can minimize or close the synced lyrics panel, and it follows the song and refreshes when you skip to the next one.

## Good to know

- **Logged out, always.** The extension never needs, reads, or touches your YouTube account. Everything works while signed out, on purpose.
- **Collects nothing.** No analytics, no phone-home. The add-on declares no data collection, and the media it fetches is requested without your cookies.
- **Firefox desktop and Android**, Firefox 128 and up.
- **Falls back gracefully.** When a video cannot be played as audio-only (live streams, made-for-kids, age-restricted, or members-only), it simply hands you back to normal YouTube. No broken playback, ever.

Give it a spin, put your headphones on, and let YouTube be the radio it always wanted to be. If something looks off, the built-in reporter makes it easy to tell us, and there is nothing personal in what it sends.
